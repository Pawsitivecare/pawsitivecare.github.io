About us

In 2021, many pet owners had work at home or they need to go back to their offices because we were already recovering from the consequences of Covid-19. This was also the year we started to give our services to those who are in need of veterinary care for their pets at the comfort of their homes.

In 2022, we decided to put up a small clinic in Marikina City that will also serve as our home base since majority or our clients were from that area. We still offer home service but for Marikina residents only.
