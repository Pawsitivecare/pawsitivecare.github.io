 Our Services

Affordable veterinary and grooming services in Marikina!

Consultation

Pet consultation is seeking professional advice from veterinarians. It helps with pet health, behavior, and well-being.

Grooming

Pet grooming is the professional care and maintenance or pet's appearance and hygiene. It includes services like bathing, brushing, haircutting, and nail trimming. Grooming keeps pets clean, comfortable, and looking their best.

Vaccination & Deworming

Vaccines help to prevent infectious diseases, while deworming treatments eliminate internal parasites like worms. These preventive measures are crucial for maintaining the health and well-being of pets.

Pharmacy

A pet pharmacy provides medications for pets, prescribed by veterinarians, to keep them healthy and treat their health conditions.

Pet Supplies

Pet supplies are essential items and products needed to care for and meet the needs of pets. They include food, toys, treats, litter, grooming tools, and other accessories. Pet supplies ensure that pets have comfortable and healthy lifestyle.

Surgery

Pet surgery is medical procedures performed on animals to diagnose, treat, or correct health conditions. It is conducted by trained veterinarians to improve pet's health and well-being.

Confinement

Confinement for pets involves restricting their movement for safety and management purposes. It includes quarantine, crate training, and temporary confinement for medical or behavioral reasons.

Ultrasound

Ultrasound for pets is a non-invasive imaging technique that uses high-frequency sound waves to create images of internal body structures and organs. It is pain-free and harmless to pets.
